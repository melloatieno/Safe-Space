Sexual abuse is the actual or attempted physical intrusion of a sexual nature, where either by force or under equal or coercive conditions. Acts of sexual exploitation and abuse include: Sexual assault, demanding sex in any context, forcing someone to have sex with anyone and forcing someone to engage in prostitution or pornography.

There is no right or wrong way to react to sexual abuse. A victim/survivor may wonder if s/he should or should not be feeling a certain way, how long s/he should be feeling it for or s/he should be over it by now.

Safe-space is here to help you understand the nitty gritties of sexual abuse and provide you with the needed support throught the health care providers and/or law enforcement agencies. In that same light upon choice a survivor/victim can connect with a counsellor whom they journey with throught the recovery.

Are you a support institution, i.e. health care provider, law enforcement agency or a therapy center, and would like to be included in the support system. Feel free to click the sign up button.
